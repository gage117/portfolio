About Me

Based in St. Paul MN, I'm a VR and Sim racing enthusiast and a passionate Full-stack Developer that works in 8 months of winter and 4 months of scorching hot not-winter.

My current experience is in HTML/CSS, JS, jQuery, and Node, but I am flexible and diverse, open to learning new languages and technologies. I will never stop learning, and never stop getting better.

I leaped into coding after reaching the advancement ceiling of my local audio industry and wanting something new. In that industry, I gained a great deal of experience in communication, customer relations, and management skills.

But I wanted to be more hands-on with tech. Since I was a kid I wondered how computers worked. Knowing coding to be much of the magic, it quickly became a fiery passion as I entered boot camp to pursue my new life and I haven't stopped learning since.
